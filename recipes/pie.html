Pie Lemonade

"When life throws pies at you, mkae yourself a tall, cool glass of Pie Lemonade!"

A refreshing summer cocktail.

Ingredients

  • Big slice of pie (apple pie, chicken pot pie, anything works)
  • Lemonade
  • (Optional) Lemon wedge

Recipe instructions

  1. Pour lemonade into a tall glass.
  2. Insert pie slice into glass.
  3. Garnish beverage with a lemon wedge
