Milk Steak

A scrumptious steak dinner, best served with free-range jelly beans

Only the finest cuts of beef should be used, and is delicious when served immediately, or after fermenting in the trash for 1 week.

Ingredients

  • Beef cut of choice (Wagyu or Kobe is ideal)
  • Cow milk, fresh and unpasteurized
  • (Optional) Vegan jelly beans

Recipe instructions

  1. Bring the fresh milk to a roaring boil
  2. Tear apart beef into palatable chunks with your hands
  3. Put beef chunks into boiling milk
  4. Let beef boil for at least 18 hours, until boiled hard
  5. Remove beef from milk and serve on your finest china with a side of raw vegan jelly beans
